Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This tangy, creamy Buffalo chicken dip tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings! Perfect for parties and game day, this chicken dip is best served hot with crackers and celery sticks. Everyone love the results!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream cheese
  • Ranch dresssing
  • Hot sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce.
  2. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheedar cheese.
  3. Cover and cook on low.
  4. Serve with celery sticks and crackers
